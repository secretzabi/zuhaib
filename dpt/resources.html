How the Written Test Works

Most states use multiple‑choice questions taken from the Driver Handbook. Expect questions on right‑of‑way, speed limits, signs and signals, lane use, and safe‑driving basics like following distance and school zones. Passing scores and time limits vary by state. Always confirm details on your state’s DMV site.

Tip: For best results, take one 50‑question practice test, a Quick Test to review weak areas, then a Mini Test for a short daily drill.

Test‑Day Checklist

  • Valid ID and required forms (as listed by your DMV)
  • Know fees and payment method; arrive a little early
  • Read each question slowly; eliminate wrong answers first
  • If allowed, review flagged questions before submitting
