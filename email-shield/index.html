How EmailShield Works

Our protection is powered by a sophisticated two-part system that ensures comprehensive threat coverage.

Massive Disposable Email Database

EmailShield continuously scrapes and monitors temporary email providers like Smail Pro, 10MinuteMail, GuerrillaMail, and more. Our system includes a custom script that constantly fetches and updates known disposable email addresses and domains via provider APIs.

Powerful Detection API

Our API is your real-time gateway to detecting:

  • Disposable email addresses: Instantly block known temporary emails.
  • Suspicious local parts: Detect bot-generated patterns like temp_user_29288@...
  • Known spammer patterns: Identify and flag addresses that match known abuse signatures.
  • Reputation scores: Get a risk score for every email address to make informed decisions.
