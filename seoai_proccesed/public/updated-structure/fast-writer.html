Define the theme and the model
Selecting a suitable writing tone is ideal for that your texts are created in the best way. Can choose between the following:
  • Informal: Your article will be written in a very general, using common words and little specialized. Recommended for articles or writings general
  • Academic: The article created will have terms technical and will provide you with sources to support the information provided. Perfect for homework and academic blogs.
  • Journalist: Perfect for blog articles informative, this writing tone will help you find more up-to-date information.
  • Formal: The text created by our tool Fast Writer will be with a formal and respectful tone, used in creating proposals or messages to attract clients of a high professional level.
  • Professional: The article created with this shade of writing will be generated with qualified terms, in addition to containing respectful and serious language. Perfect for blogs or specialized sites.
  • Vibrant: The generated text will have a fenced tone and friendly, perfect for letters or sales messages to E-commerce products.
Point of view

Define the perspective from which you want it to be written:

  • First Person: Involves the reader directly, using "I" or "we."
  • Second Person: Provides more insight objective using "he", "she" or "they".
  • Third Person: Shows the number of links that a domain is directly related to the keyword investigated
Writing style

Indicate the writing style desired in your text, the Styles that you can select are the following:

  • Narrative: Tells a story or sequence of events, ideal for creating an emotional connection with the reader.
  • Descriptive: Focuses on describing in detail people, places, objects or situations, helping paint a vivid picture in the reader's mind.
  • Persuasive: Seeks to convince the reader of an idea or point of view, using arguments and calls to the action. It is ideal for marketing content.
  • Speaker: Presents clear and objective information, explaining specific concepts or topics. It is useful for guides and tutorials.
  • Creative: Allows you to explore innovative ideas and use an artistic language, ideal for content that requires an original or unusual approach.
