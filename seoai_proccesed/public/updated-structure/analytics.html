How to use our tool
ANALYTICS WEB

Web analytics are essential because provide key information about the performance of a website and the visitor behavior. .

Analytics Web It is a tool that provides a complete analysis of the performance of your website. Below you will find a guide step by step on how to use this tool effectively
1
Login
Go to SEOAI.com and enter
your username and password
by clicking the 'Log In' button.
2
Access the Reports Section
Once logged in, go to the menu on the left side of the screen and Click on the "Analytics" option.
3
Connect SEOAI ​​to the website
To start analyzing the data of the website, you must connect SEOAI. Here we explain the two methods to do it
  • Method 1 (Insert script manually): To do it, copy the code provided by SEOAI ​​in the “analysis” section web” and insert it in the section < head >< /head > or < body >< /body > of your website. When doing so, it is It is important that you review the file manager hosting your site.
  • Method 2 (Install Plugin, available only for WordPress): Download and install the SEOAI ​​plugin that you will find in the section “web analysis”. Once installed, enter your API KEY when requested.
4
Analysis
To perform a complete analysis of the website, it is essential Enter the following information in the tool:
  • Add a Domain or Subdomain: Add the domain what you want to analyze and choose who You can see the results of the analysis (private, public or password protected).
  • Exclude IPs: If you want to exclude visits from certain IP addresses, add them, one per line.
  • Excluir Bots: Activate the option to exclude bots from scanning and get more accurate data about real users.
  • Settings Notifications: Set the email to receive periodic reports on performance of your site.
  • Start Analysis: Set the email to receive periodic reports on performance of your site.
5
Results
SEOAI.com provides various types of detailed reports to evaluate different aspects of your website and optimize it. Below the we will describe

Types of REPORTS

Overview
  • IThis report offers a comparison between the number of visitors and pages viewed during a period specific (daily, weekly, monthly). Which allows understand the popularity of content and adjust the frequency of publications or updates of your place.
Visitors by Country
  • Show the geographical origin of your visitors pages, which allows you to identify in which regions your website is having a greater impact.
  • Domain Authority (DA): It is a metric developed by Moz that measures the probability that a complete domain appears in the top positions of search engines. It is another metric broad that considers the authority of all pages of a website as a whole. It is measured within a range (1-100) with 100 being the highest probability that search engines show that website.
  • Page Authority (PA): The authority of a page (PA) is a metric developed by Moz that measures the probability that a URL appears in the search engines. It is a broader metric than considers the authority of all the pages of a site website as a whole. It is measured within a range (1-100) 100 being the highest probability that search engines show that web page.
Visitors by Operating System

Show the geographical origin of your visitors pages, which allows you to identify in which regions your website is having a greater impact.

Visitors by Browser

Show the browsers most used by your visitors (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.). This information is vital so that your site is optimized for browsers most used by your users, which will improve the user experience and SEO.

Domain References (Refer Domains)

Indicates backlinks, which direct to your site from other web pages. Additionally, it shows how many visitors arrive to your site through these links and show so percentage how the level of visits has changed with respect to to the previous month.

Visited Pages (Pages)

Shows the URLs of the most viewed pages on your site, along with the number of visits they receive regarding the previous month. This metric allows you to identify the pages most popular and optimize your content.

Website (Website):

Gives you a summary of all the pages of your site, the number of visitors they have received and the times they have been seen.

Descargar los reportes
