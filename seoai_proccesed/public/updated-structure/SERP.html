How to use our tool
SERP EVOLUTION

SERP stands for Search Engine Results Page and refers to results pages displayed by a specific search engine: usually Google, but also Bing, Baidu or Yahoo.

Our tool allows you to analyze several keywords at the same time and see which are the first 100 websites in the search results. Below you will find a guide step by step on how to use this tool effectively
1
Login
Go to SEOAI.com and enter
your username and password
by clicking the 'Log In' button.
2
Access the SERP Section Evolution
Once logged in, go to the menu on the left side of the screen and click on the "SERP's Evolution" option
3
Enter the URL, country and keywords for analysis
  • In the SERP section, paste the URL of the website you want analyze in the field corresponding.
  • Enter the country where you want focus the analysis, or, you can choose a general analysis.
  • Enter all the words or key phrases related to website and click “Analyze”

Types of REPORTS

SERP Overview Report

Shows the evolution over time of the position of the domain on Google or Bing, taking into account the Keywords provided.

  • Time filter: Our tool has a time filter with the following periods:
    1. Last 30 days
    2. Last 3 months
    3. Last 6 months
    4. Last 12 months
    5. Last 2 years
    6. Last 5 years
Keywords Report
Evaluate the keywords provided with the following metrics to facilitate analysis:
  • Ranking: It is the place where the website is located positioned according to that word. The smaller the number, the better positioning it has in the search engine selected.
  • Positioning variation: Shows the variation positioning that it has had since the beginning of the analysis up to the current period. This metric helps Identify if your SEO strategies, to position more keywords on your website are working.
  • Average: Shows the average ranking of the last 12 results tracked
  • Best Ranking: Indicates the best position reached in the last year since first analysis
  • WPR: Reflects the popularity ranking of sites website, provided by Tranco. The smaller the number is better the ranking that the website has with that word.
Domain Authority

It is a level that shows the relevance of the website during the time since your first analysis. one more level high shows that the domain has greater relevance compared to other websites. It is measured in a range (0-100) with 100 being the highest level authority and 0 being the authority lower.

Performance

Shows the performance of the analyzed domain. HE measures in a range of (0-100), with 100 being performance excellent and 0 a poor performance or, this can mean that we do not have a significant sample to provide a worth.

Keyword Position Flow

Indicates the behavior of the position of the words key provided for analysis, providing information up, down or unchanged in general

Keyword variation

You will find a table with information on the variation in the position of the analyzed keywords. It is measured according to a ranking (1 (green) - 100+ (red)) with 1 being the best ranking position.

Historial de Búsquedas:

Our tool automatically saves the searches you have generated to facilitate an analysis of previously searched terms and the sites that you want to analyze. You can download all the data in CSV format. Only you must enter the search and click on the section “Export in CSV”.
