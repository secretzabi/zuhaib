Keyword Reasearch

Keywords are essential for SEO because they act as a link between what the people search and the content you offer on your website.

Keywords Research is a tool SEOAI ​​that allows you to discover keywords high value, identify opportunities for ranking, understand your SEO status and Go one step further than the competition. Below you will find a guide to the step step by step on how you can take advantage of it maximum
1
Login
Go to SEOAI.com and enter
your username and password
by clicking the 'Log In' button.
2
Access the Reports Section
Once logged in, go to the menu on the left side of the screen and Click on the "Keywords Research" option.
3
Enter a phrase for Analysis
Enter a short phrase that define the business, where names the keyword, the country where develop, the language and click "Find Keywords" button
4
Results
SEOAI ​​will show you certain types of reports with the best words key to position your website, using relative keywords and relevant information. TO Below we will explain each one

Types of REPORTS

Keyword Report
This report provides the top keywords relevant in the country and language provided relating to your business, including:
  • Difficulty: This metric, provided by Google parameters, indicates the difficulty of position a keyword in the engines search through SEO. It is essential to understand the level of competition each word faces clue.
  • Trend: Shows search volume made with a keyword in the last year, allowing to identify patterns of popularity and seasonality in searches.
  • CPC (Cost Per Click): It is the estimated cost per click What is recommended if you want to run a campaign search engine advertising using that keyword. This metric is crucial for planning an effective marketing budget.
  • Volume: Indicates the daily search volume which generates a keyword. This information is vital to understanding the potential traffic that could attract to your website.
SERP Report

By clicking on a keyword in the list, you will be able to see the domains that appear in the top ten places of search engines for that keyword specific and you will find:

  • Autoridad del dominio (DA): It is a metric developed by Moz that measures the probability that a complete domain appears in the top positions of search engines. It is measured within a range (1-100) with 100 being the highest probability that search engines show that website.
  • Page Authority (PA): The authority of a page (PA) is a metric developed by Moz that measures the probability that a URL appears in the search engines. It is measured within a range (1- 100) with 100 being the highest probability that the search engines show that web page.
  • Links: Shows the number of links that a domain is directly related to the keyword investigated
  • Additional Plus: You will be able to export these reports, so that you can carry out a more detailed analysis and consult the history of the reports generated in your account

Integrating Keywords Research into your SEO strategy can transform the visibility of your website, helping you attract more traffic and improve your search engine rankings.
