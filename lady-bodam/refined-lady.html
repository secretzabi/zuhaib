After graduating from university, I moved back to Nigeria to start building my career. I was doing well and occasionally got invited to speak to groups of women. My social life was amazing, my career was growing, and I had a steady romantic relationship (or several romantic relationships, but that's a story for another day... over a glass of wine). Everything seemed to be on track.

However, in 2018, I woke up with an "itch" - a feeling of dissatisfaction and restlessness that I couldn't shake off. I didn't know what was causing it, but I felt like there had to be more to life than what I was currently experiencing.

I thought that getting another job or a promotion would solve my problem, but of course, I was wrong. I moved to a more senior role in March 2019, and for the first two months, I felt at peace. However, in May 2019, the feelings of restlessness and unease hit me like a ton of bricks.
