Modern Slavery Statement

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

Company Name: TempCatcher Ltd.

At TempCatcher, we are committed to conducting business with integrity and transparency. We fully oppose all forms of modern slavery, human trafficking, forced labor, and exploitation. This statement outlines the steps we take to ensure that modern slavery is not taking place within our organization or our supply chains.

Our Business

TempCatcher is a technology company providing advanced email spam detection solutions to enterprise customers. Our systems analyze both the domain and local-part of email addresses to identify temporary, disposable, or high-risk usage patterns that indicate spam or abuse.

As a digital-first company, our operations are based primarily in office and remote work environments, and we do not engage in manufacturing or high-risk labor sectors.

Our Commitment

  • We do not tolerate modern slavery or human trafficking in any part of our business.
  • We expect all our partners, contractors, and suppliers to uphold the same zero-tolerance standards.
  • We work only with reputable providers and service partners who share our ethical values and operate in accordance with applicable labor laws.

Due Diligence & Risk Management

Although TempCatcher operates in a low-risk sector for modern slavery, we maintain a responsible approach to vendor selection and risk assessment. This includes:

  • Regular reviews of key suppliers and partners
  • Ensuring our contracts include compliance expectations
  • Internal policies promoting transparency and fair treatment

Employee Awareness

All employees and contractors at TempCatcher are made aware of our commitment to ethical business practices and are encouraged to report any concerns or violations of this policy, including signs of labor exploitation.

Continuous Improvement

We understand that addressing modern slavery requires ongoing vigilance. TempCatcher is committed to reviewing and strengthening our practices annually to reflect best industry standards and legal requirements.

This statement is made in accordance with the UK Modern Slavery Act 2015 and is approved by the directors of TempCatcher Ltd.
