3 Powerful Tools To Identify Implants

AI-Identify (available now for hips)

  • Upload an unknown hip x-ray and see the top implant matches from our library.
  • Confirm your match to help our AI model improve!

Implant Library with Filters

  • Browse and search from hundreds of common spine or orthopedic implant x-rays.
  • Use advanced filters to narrow your search. 
  • Tap the company name to see removal information when available.

Ask the Community

  • Share unknown implant x-rays to the Home Feed.
  • Receive real-time feedback and implant suggestions from other surgeons and reps worldwide.
  • Share your implant suggestions and upvote the best responses to help others in the community.

How It Works

1. Tap + to begin

  • Select an image from your gallery or use your camera to take a photo of your implant.
  • Crop your image with 2 fingers, so only the implant is in view.

2. Select your method

  • I-Identify provides instant library identification when available.
  • Ask the community to post your implant to the home feed.
  • Submit new implants to be added to our implant library.

3. Confirm your match

  • Every implant you confirm, helps our AI model improve!
