Our mission

At Implant Identifier, our mission is simple:
To create the world’s most intelligent implant identification platform.

The story behind Implant Identifier

As surgeons and reps, we’ve all been there — an unfamiliar implant on x-ray, a revision case with no prior op note, a surgery done in another city or country. Tracking down the implant, manufacturer, or removal tools can be a frustrating, time-consuming process.

Implant Identifier was created in 2018 by orthopedic spine surgeon Dr. Parth Desai to help answer a common question faced by spine surgeons, orthopedic surgeons and device reps everyday:

“What implant is this?”

Dr. Desai envisioned an AI-based approach to accurately identify any orthopedic and spine implant based on the x-ray alone. What started as a simple x-ray library, has developed into a community of over 50,000 surgeons and reps worldwide.

🔐 Privacy features

  • We use a proprietary text-removal AI model on your device to ensure no protected health information (PHI) is transmitted through the app.

  • All users must certify that all x-rays are de-identified and that they have the authority to upload the image.

  • Our support team purges any images in our library found to be unauthorized and bans users for violation of our terms.

  • Our backend infrastructure follows HIPAA security standards, though we do not permit the storage or use of any PHI and therefore are not bound by HIPAA regulation.
